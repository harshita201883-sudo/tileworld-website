Kitchen Tiles Information

1. Where Are Kitchen Tiles Used?
🔷Backsplash – Behind the stove or sink for easy cleaning and style.
🔷Flooring – Needs to be durable and easy to clean.
🔷 Walls – Especially around cooking and sink areas.
🔷 Countertops – Less common now, but possible with certain tiles..

2. Tile Sizes for Kitchen :
🔷 Backsplash: Small tiles (3"x6" subway, mosaic sheets)
🔷 Flooring: Larger tiles (12"x12", 18"x18", or 12"x24")
🔷 Wall feature: Medium to large tiles (6"x6" to 12"x24")

3. Design Tips
🔷 Backsplash: Try subway tiles, Moroccan patterns, or geometric mosaics.
🔷 Floor: Use slip-resistant matte tiles in neutral tones or stone look.
🔷 Contrast: Pair bold backsplash tiles with plain countertops/cabinets.
🔷 Accent wall: Add personality with a tile wall around a dining nook or coffee station.
