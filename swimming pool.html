Swinning Pool Tiles Information

1. Why Tiles for Pools?
🔷 Water-resistant & chemical-proof
🔷 Durable under UV and weather
🔷 Easy to clean
🔷 Resistant to algae (especially glass tiles)
🔷 Great for custom patterns and visual effects

2. Installation Tips
🔷 Use epoxy-based grout for durability and waterproofing.
🔷 Ensure professional waterproofing beneath the tile layer.
🔷 Add grip strips or use textured tiles on steps and edges.
