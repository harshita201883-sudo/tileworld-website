Sanitary Ware Tiles Information

1. Sanitary Ware Materials:
🔷 Vitreous China: Most common; strong, glossy, non-porous.
🔷 Ceramic: Similar to vitreous china, smooth and easy to clean.
🔷 Stainless Steel: Durable, mostly used in public spaces.
🔷 Acrylic: Lightweight, used in bathtubs and vanity tops.

2. Matching Sanitary Ware & Tiles :
🔷 White sanitary ware = Matches almost any tile color.
🔷 For luxury bathrooms: Try marble-look tiles with matte black or grey basins.
🔷 Minimalist design: Pair simple white sanitary ware with neutral beige or stone-textured tiles.
🔷 Modern look: Use dark floor tiles with contrasting light wall tiles.
