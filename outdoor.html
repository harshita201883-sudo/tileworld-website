Out Door Tiles Information

1. Design Tips for Outdoor Tiles
🔷 Use stone-look or textured tiles for a natural garden feel.
🔷 For modern patios, go with large-format porcelain in grey or concrete tones.
🔷 Create pathways with different colored tiles or patterns.
🔷 Border the tiles with grass or pebbles for a landscape vibe.
🔷 Add warm lighting and planters to complete the look.

2. Maintenance Tips :
🔷 Sweep regularly to avoid moss or dirt buildup.
🔷 Use pressure washing for deep cleaning (especially stone or concrete).
🔷 Reseal natural stones or terracotta every 1–2 years.
