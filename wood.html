Wood Tiles Information

1. What Are Wood Tiles?
Wood-look tiles are tiles made from ceramic or porcelain that are designed to look like natural wood — including grain patterns, knots, and textures.They combine the aesthetic of wood with the durability of tile, making them perfect for indoor and outdoor spaces.

2. Advantages of Wood Tiles:
✅ Water-resistant (unlike real wood)
✅ Scratch and stain-resistant
✅ Long-lasting and low maintenance
✅ Looks almost identical to real wood
✅ Great for underfloor heating (if needed)
