Bathroom Tiles Information

1. Ceramic Tiles :Affordable, easy to install and clean.Good for walls and light-traffic floors.

2. Porcelain Tiles : Denser and more water-resistant than ceramic.Ideal for floors, especially in wet areas

3. Mosaic Tiles :Small tiles made of ceramic, glass, or stone.Great for accents, shower floors, or backsplashes

4. Natural Stone Tiles :Includes marble, granite, slate, travertine.Luxurious but needs sealing and maintenance

5. Standard sizes : 12"x12", 18"x18", 24"x24"

6. Subway tiles: 3"x6

7. Mosaic tiles: 1"x1", 2"x2"

8. Large format: 12"x24" or bigger for a sleek, modern loo
