Cladding Tiles Information

1. What Are Cladding Tiles?
🔷 Cladding tiles are used to cover and decorate walls—both interior and exterior—to provide a decorative, protective layer. They can mimic natural materials (like stone or brick) and are popular for giving walls a textured or stylish finish

2. Uses of Cladding Tiles
🔷 Exterior walls (house fronts, boundary walls)
🔷 Interior accent walls (living rooms, bathrooms, kitchens)
🔷 Pillars and columns
🔷 Fireplace surrounds
🔷 Facade designs

3. Stone Cladding :Natural stone like slate, sandstone, granite.Heavy, durable, and gives a rustic or luxurious look.

4. Brick Cladding:Mimics real brick walls.Great for vintage or industrial themes.
